## Rules

**Doors and access:** After 5 PM, venue doors are locked from the outside. If stepping out, make sure someone inside knows to let you back in.

**Code of conduct:** No harassment of any kind (verbal, physical, online). Coordinators may remove anyone who doesn't comply. Report concerns to a coordinator immediately.

**Common areas:** Keep time in the lobby brief — congestion affects others.

### Sleeping
- Sleeping bag *(contact organizer ASAP if you don't have one)*

- Sleep mask

### Showering *(showers on site)*
